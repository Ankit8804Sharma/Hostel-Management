Powerful Features for Every Role

From filing a complaint to booking a gaming console — all hostel operations in one system.

Complaints

Students file complaints by category. Wardens assign staff to resolve issues. Real-time status tracking from Open to Resolved.

Laundry

Submit laundry requests with item details and weight. Track progress from Pending to Delivered without leaving the portal.

Gaming Booking

Browse available gaming equipment, book it instantly, and return with one click. Availability updates in real time.

Attendance

Wardens mark daily attendance. Students see their Present / Absent / Leave summary and full 30-day history at a glance.

Task Management

Wardens assign tasks to staff members. Staff can mark tasks as Completed with a single button, keeping everyone updated.

Room Allocation

Wardens manage room assignments with full visibility of allotted and vacated rooms across AC and Non-AC categories.
